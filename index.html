About Me

Hi! My name is Lorenzo, I'm 22 and a French student at the ECE Paris-Lyon engineering school, where I'm specializing in embedded systems, a fusion of electronics and computer science. My studies are a combination of theory and practice, giving me a solid base in these fields. As a result, I have acquired knowledge of both the hardware and software sides of electronics, as well as taking part in concrete projects that have consolidated my skills.

Besides my studies, I'm passionate about aerospace, new technologies and photography. As a member of the ECE Space association, I have contributed to a number of space-related projects. I'm also a member of the JBTV association, where I can be a photographer at events organized by my school.

In addition, I have developed this portfolio using HTML, CSS and JavaScript, to improve my web development skills. I'm also learning graphic design using tools such as Adobe Illustrator, Premiere Pro and After Effects, adding a creative dimension to my projects.
