About Me

Hi! My name is Lorenzo, I'm 22 and a French student at the ECE Paris-Lyon engineering school, where I'm specializing in embedded systems, a fusion of electronics and computer science. My studies are a combination of theory and practice, giving me a solid base in these fields.
